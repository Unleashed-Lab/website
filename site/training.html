We provide multiple types of training services.

In-person, hosted by Unleashed Lab

We are in the process of creating course content focused on reverse engineering "new age" targets, such as malware written in Swift, Go, or Rust.

We expect to launch these in-person courses in late 2024 or early 2025. Stay tuned or contact us to book early seats.

Private, customer-focused, on-site

If you want to host a private training session internal to your company, we can come to you. We can deliver training sessions on various topics related to the services we offer or we can partner with you to deliver custom or more advanced content.

Note: we do not provide online or video-conference training as the experience simply is not the same. That said, if you want to record our session for future internal use, we can certainly support that.

Train the trainer, customer-owned

If you have a need to develop training materials specific to and owned by your company, we can partner together to build your content. Once the content is developed using templates of your choice, we will follow a "train the trainer" model delivering the content to your team members who would be expected to deliver it going forward.
